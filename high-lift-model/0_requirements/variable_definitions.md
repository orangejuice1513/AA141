## Variable definitions (fill these in early)

Primary independent variables:
- \(\alpha\): angle of attack [deg]. Define sign convention and reference line.
- \(\delta_f\): trailing-edge flap deflection [deg]. Define positive direction.
- \(\delta_s\): leading-edge device deflection/setting [deg or discrete setting]. Define positive direction.

Primary output:
- \(C_L\): wing lift coefficient. Define reference area \(S\) and whether coefficients are wing-level or section-level.

Recommended conventions (edit if your class uses different):
- Angles in **degrees** in plots/tables; internally you can convert later if needed.
- Use wing planform area \(S\) for \(C_L\) when you move to wing-level results.
