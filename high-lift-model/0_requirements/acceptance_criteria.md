## Acceptance criteria (what you must be able to produce)

Required plots/tables (both **clean** and **takeoff**):
- \(C_L\) vs \(\alpha\) (at representative \(\delta_f,\delta_s\) for that config)

Required “control authority” plots (at a few fixed \(\alpha\) values you choose):
- \(C_L\) vs \(\delta_f\) at fixed \(\delta_s\)
- \(C_L\) vs \(\delta_s\) at fixed \(\delta_f\)

Combined model:
- Provide a way to evaluate \(C_L(\alpha,\delta_f,\delta_s)\)
- Show at least one visualization of the 3-variable model (surface or multiple slices)

Documentation:
- Every curve/value used from Raymer is logged in `1_data/raw/raymer_ch12/citations.md`.
